I love drawing mazes.
My artist name, May, comes from "mei", from the Japanese word "meiro" meaning "maze".
I draw intricate mazes by hand, based on mathematical formulas and patterns that attract me.
I draw mazes simply because I enjoy the act of drawing itself, thinking to myself, "This is beautiful."
When I meet someone who finds beauty in the mazes I drew believing they were beautiful, it makes me very happy, and I feel truly glad.
Keeping that feeling in mind, I want to draw many more beautiful mazes.
